Dead-simple, responsive site layout.

Webdev is also a form of art in itself. Art is a form of expression. I wanted to express my feelings through Pico — how I want things to be simple and neat, how I want the elements to be consistent, how I want the site to behave similarly regardless of the browser — amidst all my discontentment in current practices.

To start working on a Pico project, simply clone the repository to a local directory using Git.
